A Better Camera short Feature list

ABC provides wide range of modes and access to all settings of your camera.

You will find general tips on next page!

Main controls

Bottom controls

Left control - fast access to your latest image in the gallery/editor. 

Center control - shutter to start/stop capture.

Right control - access to mode selection.

Top controls

Top menu shows fast controls. Pull it down to see all quick controls:

Scenes, white balance, ISO, flash, focus, exposure metering and more!

Select "more settings" to get access to all settings - general, viewfinder, capture and processing modes, export.

More settings

When in settings - you will find different sections to access different application settings:

General settings

Access to all image sizes in any modes - general image size, multishot and night image size, panorama image size.

+ volume button and headset button settings, quality settings...

Viewfinder settings

Settings in this section allow to configure controls on main screen and manage screen behavior.

You can add/hide self timer, exposure and white balance locks, zoom control or add different useful info controls
